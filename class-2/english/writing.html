✏️ Writing Skills

ବାକ୍ୟ ଗଠନ, ଅନୁଚ୍ଛେଦ ଓ ଚିଠି ଲେଖିବା ଶିଖନ୍ତୁ — Learn to form sentences, write paragraphs and letters!

📐 Forming Sentences
Learn to make correct English sentences
📘 What is a Sentence?
A sentence is a group of words that makes complete sense. Every sentence starts with a capital letter and ends with a full stop (.), question mark (?) or exclamation mark (!).
ବାକ୍ୟ ହେଉଛି ଶବ୍ଦଗୁଡ଼ିକର ଗୋଟିଏ ସମୂହ ଯାହା ସମ୍ପୂର୍ଣ୍ଣ ଅର୍ଥ ଦେଇଥାଏ। ପ୍ରତ୍ୟେକ ବାକ୍ୟ ବଡ଼ ଅକ୍ଷରରେ ଆରମ୍ଭ ହୁଏ ଓ ପୂର୍ଣ୍ଣବିରାମ, ପ୍ରଶ୍ନବୋଧକ ବା ବିସ୍ମୟସୂଚକ ଚିହ୍ନରେ ଶେଷ ହୁଏ।
Remember
A sentence needs: Who/What (subject) + Does what (verb) + More info (object)
Examples
The cat sits on the mat.
My mother cooks food.
I go to school every day.
Birds fly in the sky.
We play in the park.
❓ Types of Sentences
1. Statements — tell something. End with a full stop (.)
2. Questions — ask something. End with a question mark (?)
3. Exclamations — show surprise or strong feelings. End with an exclamation mark (!)
Examples
The sun is bright. (Statement)
Where is my book? (Question)
What a beautiful flower! (Exclamation)
📝 Writing Paragraphs
How to write a simple paragraph
📘 What is a Paragraph?
A paragraph is a group of sentences about one topic. Start with a topic sentence that tells the main idea. Then add 2-3 sentences with more details.
ଅନୁଚ୍ଛେଦ ହେଉଛି ଗୋଟିଏ ବିଷୟ ବାବଦରେ ଲେଖାଯାଇଥିବା ବାକ୍ୟ ସମୂହ। ପ୍ରଥମ ବାକ୍ୟରେ ମୁଖ୍ୟ ଧାରଣା ଲେଖନ୍ତୁ। ତାପରେ ୨-୩ ଟି ବାକ୍ୟରେ ଅଧିକ ବିବରଣୀ ଦିଅନ୍ତୁ।
Example Paragraph: My School
My school is very nice. It has many rooms and a big playground. My teacher is kind and helpful. I love going to school every day. My friends and I play together during break time.
Example Paragraph: My Pet
I have a pet dog named Tommy. He is brown and very friendly. He likes to run in the garden. I give him food and water every day. Tommy makes me very happy.
✉️ Letter Writing
How to write a simple letter
📘 Parts of a Letter
A simple letter has these parts:
1. Your address and date (top right)
2. Greeting: Dear ___ (who you are writing to)
3. Body: What you want to say
4. Closing: Yours lovingly / Your friend
5. Your name
ଏକ ସରଳ ଚିଠିର ଅଂଶଗୁଡ଼ିକ: ଠିକଣା ଓ ତାରିଖ, ଅଭିବାଦନ, ମୂଳ ବିଷୟ, ସମାପ୍ତି ଓ ନାମ।
Example Letter: To a Friend
My Home
Bhubaneswar
10 March 2026

Dear Raju,

How are you? I am fine. I miss you very much. We had a sports day at our school last week. I won the running race! I wish you were there.

Please come to visit me during the holidays. We will play together and have fun.

Your friend,
Sita
Writing Tips
1. Always write neatly
2. Use capital letters at the start of sentences and for names
3. End sentences with the correct punctuation mark
4. Leave space between paragraphs
