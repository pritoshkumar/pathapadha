Composition

Story Writing & Picture Description

📘 Marigold Part 3 (NCERT)

Learn to write short stories and describe pictures in English. Good writing uses clear sentences, interesting details, and a proper beginning, middle, and end.

📖 How to Write a Short Story
Every good story has three parts: 1️⃣ Beginning — Introduce the characters and setting Who is in the story? Where does it happen? 2️⃣ Middle — Tell what happens (the problem or event) What is the main event? What goes wrong? 3️⃣ End — How is the problem solved? How does the story finish? What did the character learn?
Example Story: The Lost Puppy Beginning: Meena found a small puppy near the park. It looked lost and hungry. Middle: Meena took the puppy home and gave it milk and bread. She made posters saying "Found: Small Brown Puppy" and put them around the neighbourhood. End: The next day, a little boy came to her house. "That is my puppy, Bruno!" he said happily. Meena was glad she helped.
🖼️ How to Describe a Picture
When describing a picture, answer these questions: 1️⃣ What do you see? — Name the people, animals, or things 2️⃣ Where is it? — Describe the place (garden, school, market) 3️⃣ What is happening? — Describe the action 4️⃣ How does it feel? — Is it happy, busy, calm, exciting? 📌 Use describing words (adjectives): big, small, happy, beautiful, sunny, colourful, busy
Example: Describe a picture of children playing in a park I can see a beautiful park with tall green trees. Three children are playing on the swings. A boy is running with his dog on the grass. The sky is blue and sunny. There are colourful flowers near the path. Everyone looks happy and excited. It is a lovely day to play outside!
✏️ Writing Tips
📌 Tips for better writing: 1. Start every sentence with a capital letter 2. End every sentence with a full stop (.), question mark (?), or exclamation mark (!) 3. Use describing words to make your writing interesting 4. Write in complete sentences, not just words 5. Read your writing aloud to check if it sounds right 6. Use "First", "Then", "Next", "Finally" to order events
🎯 Practice Topics
Try writing about these topics: 📝 My Best Friend — Who are they? What do you do together? 📝 A Rainy Day — What happened? How did it feel? 📝 My Favourite Animal — What does it look like? Why do you like it? 📝 A Visit to the Market — What did you see? What did you buy? 📝 My School — What does it look like? What is your favourite part?
