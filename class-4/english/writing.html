Diary Entry
📓
FORMAT
How to Write a Diary Entry
A personal record of your day
A diary entry has these parts: 1. Date — written at the top 2. Greeting — "Dear Diary," 3. Body — what happened, how you felt 4. Closing — sign off with your name
Sample Diary Entry
Monday, 15 July 2025 Dear Diary, Today was a wonderful day! We went on a school trip to the museum. I saw dinosaur bones and old coins. My favourite part was the science section where we saw a model of the solar system. I learned so many new things. I wish every day could be like this! Good night, Priya
Notice Writing
📋
FORMAT
How to Write a Notice
An official announcement
A notice has these parts: 1. Heading — "NOTICE" at the top 2. Date — below the heading 3. Title — what the notice is about 4. Body — important details (what, when, where) 5. Signature — name and designation
Sample Notice
NOTICE Date: 20 August 2025 ANNUAL SPORTS DAY All students are informed that the Annual Sports Day will be held on 28 August 2025 at 9:00 AM on the school playground. Students should wear their house T-shirts and white shoes. Parents are welcome to attend. Ravi Kumar Head Boy, Class 4
Informal Letter
✉️
FORMAT
How to Write an Informal Letter
A letter to a friend or family member
An informal letter has these parts: 1. Your address — top right corner 2. Date — below your address 3. Greeting — "Dear [Name]," 4. Body — your message in paragraphs 5. Closing — "Yours lovingly," or "Your friend," 6. Your name
Sample Informal Letter
45, Station Road Cuttack, Odisha 10 September 2025 Dear Anu, How are you? I hope you are doing well. I am writing to tell you about my new school. It is very big and has a beautiful garden. My teachers are kind and helpful. We have a library with hundreds of books. I have already made many friends. I miss you a lot. Please come to visit me during the holidays. Give my regards to Uncle and Aunty. Your loving friend, Sita
